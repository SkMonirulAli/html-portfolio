The Blockbuster Hindi Movies for a Movie buff

My top 3 Hindi movies of all-time.

3 Idiots" (2009)


Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, this comedy-drama revolves around three engineering students and their journey through college. The film humorously addresses the pressures and expectations of the Indian education system while advocating for pursuing one's passion and individuality.

Taare Zameen Par (2007)


Directed by Aamir Khan, this heartwarming drama explores the life of an 8-year-old dyslexic child named Ishaan and his struggles in school. The film sensitively portrays the importance of understanding and nurturing the unique talents and abilities of every child.

Lagaan(2001)


Directed by Ashutosh Gowariker, this epic sports drama is set during the British colonial rule in India. The film follows a group of villagers who accept a cricket match challenge from the British officers to avoid paying exorbitant taxes, showcasing themes of unity, resilience, and defiance.
